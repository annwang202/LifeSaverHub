Center for Cardiac Arrest Survival Volunteer Application

Thank you for your interest in volunteering with the Center for Cardiac Arrest Survival (CCAS). Our team at CCAS works hard to empower communities by teaching lifesaving skills such as CPR, AED, Stop the Bleed!, and Narcan administration. For any questions or concerns, please email ccas@pitt.edu.

To volunteer with CCAS, you must have:
  • Act 33 Clearance (CY113): https://www.compass.state.pa.us/cwis/public/home (click on create individual account) we ask that you forward your results to us at ccas@pitt.edu.
  • Act 34 Clearance (EPATCH)- we handle this one
